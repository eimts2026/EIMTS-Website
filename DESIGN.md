# Design System

## Direction

A cinematic global-passage brand experience: editorial typography, deep emerald surfaces, warm amber action color, real human imagery, generous whitespace, and asymmetric composition.

## Tokens

- Ink: #10221a
- Emerald: #063f31
- Emerald dark: #03271f
- Amber: #f3b84b
- Neutral: #f4f5f0
- White: #ffffff
- Radius: 0, 12px, and full-pill only
- Motion: 220ms interactions; 700ms chapter reveals using transform and opacity

## Typography

Roboto Flex is used across display, interface, and body copy, with optical sizing enabled and weight providing the hierarchy. Display tracking never tighter than -0.04em.

## Layout

Content width 1180px with responsive 20px to 40px gutters. Sections use 96px to 144px vertical rhythm. The hero targets two headline lines on desktop and keeps both CTAs visible on a small laptop.

## Components

Buttons use a nested circular arrow affordance. Lists stay open and typographic rather than becoming repeated cards. Testimonials use one dominant quote with supporting voices. Focus rings use amber with sufficient offset.

## Accessibility

WCAG 2.2 AA contrast, semantic headings, 48px targets, visible focus, alt text, reduced motion, no horizontal overflow, and mobile layouts tested from 375px.
