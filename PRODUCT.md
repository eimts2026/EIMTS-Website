# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Sri Lankan job seekers pursuing responsible overseas employment are the primary audience. International employers seeking carefully screened talent are the secondary audience.

## Product Purpose

Emerald Isle Manpower & Travel Services connects talent with trusted global employers and supports recruitment, documentation, and travel as one accountable journey.

## Positioning

A licensed, award-recognised human capital partner combining global reach with personal guidance since 1995.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: explore foreign jobs.
- Secondary CTA: hire talent or start a conversation.
- Memorable line: Your next chapter starts beyond borders.
- Proof: SLBFE License No. 1162, seven highlighted recognitions, 30+ years of service, regional partner offices, and client testimonials.

## Brand Personality

Assured, human, and worldly. The experience should feel ambitious without hype and premium without becoming distant.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic job-board templates, tourist-travel imagery, crowded card grids, decorative glass effects, and staged-handshake recruitment clichés.

## Design Principles

- Make global opportunity feel clear and attainable.
- Show proof through real history, credentials, and client voices.
- Give candidates and employers equally legible journeys.
- Use confident editorial pacing with human imagery.
- Keep every interaction direct, accessible, and trustworthy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA with visible focus, semantic structure, keyboard-safe navigation, reduced-motion support, strong contrast, and responsive layouts from 375px upward.
